Here is how you should run our program to view all of its functions:

-After starting the program enter "L" to go to the Login Screen

-Use an email & password from Login.csv to login to the system

-Use the "help" command to pull up a list of available commands

-Use getAccountDetails to review your account details

-Use setAccountDetails to change the desired details of your account

-Use products to view the list of available products

-use AddToOrder to add a product from the list of available products to your cart

-Use ShowCart to view all items in your cart and their price

-Use payment to pay for your order and recieve a summary of the cost and discount applied.

-Use quit to exit the program
